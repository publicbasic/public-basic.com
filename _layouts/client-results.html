Telegraph Media Group

User research, A/B testing, UX Design

The Telegraph is a daily broadsheet newspaper published in London. Founded 160 years ago, the newspaper maintains a hugely dedicated following of readers and receives 100m visitors to their website every month.

We were engaged to support a newly established Product and UX function by developing an in-house optimisation program.

This work involved:

  • Defining roles, processes and governance for test development, QA, release and results distribution
  • Working with stakeholders across the business to design experiments to give useful data quickly
  • Set-up a framework for test planning and reporting documentation and distribution.

We also achieved some pretty great test results:

  • 8% increase in subscription sales
  • 22% increase in user engagement
  • 14% lift in video plays
